FAQ

Common Questions Before Booking Private Hibachi

These are some of the most common questions customers ask before submitting a booking request.

Do you cook indoors?

No. Hibachi service is usually best for outdoor setups unless a venue clearly allows an indoor arrangement.

What do I need to provide?

You usually provide tables, chairs, utensils, and enough space. The chef brings the grill, cooking setup, and food service based on the booking.

What if my city is not listed?

Contact us anyway. We serve most cities across North Carolina, South Carolina, and Virginia and can usually still accommodate your event.
